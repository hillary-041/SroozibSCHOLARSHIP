About Sroozib Scholarships Sroozib Scholarships is an educational support organization established on 22 May 2012 in Machakos, Kenya. The organization was founded by Hillary Kishapui, Amon Tanui, and Rennis Bett with the objective of expanding access to education for academically capable and deserving students who face financial constraints. The organization was formed in response to the growing need for structured financial assistance to support students in achieving their educational and professional aspirations. Since its establishment, Sroozib Scholarships has remained committed to promoting academic excellence, equity, and social advancement through sustainable scholarship programs. Sroozib Scholarships operates on the principles of transparency, accountability, and merit-based support, ensuring that beneficiaries are selected through fair and credible processes. The organization continues to collaborate with educational institutions and stakeholders to create long-term educational opportunities that contribute to national and global development.
